Twilight Skin for Winamp
======================================

Twilight is a Winamp Skin with the
main character from Serial Experiments 
Lain; Lain, duh! The Skin is named 
after a song by BOA - the group that
sings the intro for Lain: Duvet. 
The song Twilight is far different 
from anything in the anime: it's a 
song about a love triangle. Regardless
both the anime and the band are great,
so go out and BUY (not steal) both.
